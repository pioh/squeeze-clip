SqueezeClip is a focused Android video compressor for people who want results, not a maze of folders and broken pickers.

What it does:

- shows recent camera videos first
- lets you pick actual video files instead of entire folders
- compresses clips locally on-device
- saves the result next to the original as *_tg.mp4
- shows progress, speed, and before/after size
- opens the original or compressed clip directly
- lets you mark finished clips and share them to Telegram or through the normal Android share sheet

It is designed around a simple mobile flow:

pick a clip, compress it, check the result, send it.

No cloud upload. No account. No path typing circus.
