SqueezeClip is a focused Android video compressor for people who want results, not a maze of folders and broken pickers.

What it does:

<ul>
<li>shows recent camera videos first</li>
<li>lets you pick actual video files instead of entire folders</li>
<li>compresses clips locally on-device</li>
<li>saves the result next to the original as *_tg.mp4</li>
<li>shows progress, speed, and before/after size</li>
<li>opens the original or compressed clip directly</li>
<li>lets you mark finished clips and share them to Telegram or through the normal Android share sheet</li>
</ul>

It is designed around a simple mobile flow: pick a clip, compress it, check the result, send it.

No cloud upload. No account. No path typing circus.
